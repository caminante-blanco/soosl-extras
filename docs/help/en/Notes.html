Notes

Signs in a dictionary ››

A sign can have notes. Notes tell other information about the sign. There are different types of information in notes:

  • Who uses it or when (more than is indicated by the dialect).
  • How to use the sign. For example, only in informal situations among friends. 
  • How the sign's meaning is different from other signs.
  • How you make the sign differently from other signs.
  • Where the sign comes from.
  • Facts about the person or thing the sign refers to.
  • Working notes to yourself: things you want to change before you publish the dictionary.

Notes appear under the senses and example sentences. 

In SooSL Desktop, if there are no notes for a sign, you will see ??? in place of the notes. That is a reminder: maybe you want to add a note.

In SooSL Desktop, you can add and edit notes.

In some dictionaries, there are extra videos that say the same thing as the notes.
