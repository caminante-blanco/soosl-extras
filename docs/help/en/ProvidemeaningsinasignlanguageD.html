Provide meanings in a sign language (D)

Other ways to use SooSL (D) ››

Most dictionaries show the meanings of signs using glosses in written languages. However, maybe you want to show the meanings of the sign using other signs. 

There are two things you might want to do:

  • Provide definitions in the same sign language. Then you will be making a monolingual dictionary.
  • Provide glosses in a different sign language. For each sign in one sign language, you show how to sign that meaning in another sign language. Then you will be making a bilingual dictionary, with two sign languages.

You can do these things by adding extra videos that show the definitions or glosses. Doing this is sometimes awkward, but it is possible.
