Exit SooSL

Get started with SooSL ››

When you are done working with SooSL, the easiest way to quit is to just close the SooSL window, or, in a browser, close the SooSL tab. We assume you know how to do this; it depends on your device, your operating system, whether you are using SooSL Web or SooSL Desktop, etc. 

But, there are some extra things to know about when you exit SooSL:

  • In SooSL Web:
    • If you are logged in, you can come back soon and you will still be logged in. After a period of time, you will be automatically logged out. You will have to log in again if you want to see private dictionaries. or manage a dictionary you own.
  • In SooSL Desktop:
    • You can also click Tools in the top-right corner and then Close SooSL.
    • Any changes you have made to the dictionary are saved automatically.
