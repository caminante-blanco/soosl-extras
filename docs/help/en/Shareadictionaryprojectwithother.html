Share a dictionary project with other people (D)

Manage dictionary projects (D) ››

There are two ways to share your dictionary with other people:

  • Export the dictionary project to a ZooZL file, and give it to people to import it into SooSL on their computer.
  • Publish a dictionary in SooSL Web. After you publish it the first time, you can upload a new version.
