Video playback

Signs in a dictionary ››

The video of the sign is in the center of the SooSL screen. The controls are different in SooSL Web and SooSL Desktop.


SooSL Web                          

SooSL Desktop

      Hover over the video or click it to show the controls


  • play/pause: start and stop the video
  • timecodes (W): current frame / length of whole video
  • slider: which frame of the video is currently showing
  • full screen (W): enlarge the video to fill the screen (ESC to restore)
  • speed: slow down or speed up the playback


In SooSL Desktop, you can add new signs or replace the video for a sign.
