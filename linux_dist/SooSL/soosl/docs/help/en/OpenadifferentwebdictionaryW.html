Open a different web dictionary (W)

SooSL Web offers several different dictionaries. When you are done looking at one, and want to look at another one, click Dictionaries in the menu (top gray) bar. 

On a phone or other narrow screen, click to open the menu and click Dictionaries.

You will see the startup screen again. Then you can choose a different dictionary.
