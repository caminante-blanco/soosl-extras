General information about a dictionary

The whole dictionary ››

Besides all the signs, a SooSL dictionary project contains some general information, such as: 

  • title of the dictionary
  • what signed and written languages it includes
  • who made it
  • its version number and when it was last changed
  • how many signs, senses, and glosses are in it

You can see this information in both SooSL Web and SooSL Desktop:

  • In SooSL Web, click  About dictionary at the top of the screen; you will find it in the top-right corner or in the menu (depending on how many columns are showing). 
  • In SooSL Desktop, click   Tools >  About dictionary.

You will see something like this. There is lots of information.

In SooSL Desktop, you can change this information. See the separate help topic about editing general information about a dictionary project. That topic also explains more about each type of information.
